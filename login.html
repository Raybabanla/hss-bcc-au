Protect yourself against scams

To protect yourself against fraud and scams, here are some useful tips:

- Never give remote access to your computer to anyone you don’t trust.
- Never share your passwords, one-time SMS codes or any secure information with anyone.
- Beware of being pressured to make financial decisions and transferring funds quickly.
- If an offer is unexpected or too good to be true, it probably is, so ignore it!
- Check your statements regularly and look out for any suspicious transactions.

If you think you may have been the victim of a scam, your bank details have been compromised, or you're worried about fraud on your account, please contact HSBC immediately. More information and support can be found here.
